DISCOVERING INTELLIGENCE VIA COMPUTATION

I am a researcher at Mila (Quebec AI Institute) interested in the learning aspects of AI, especially reinforcement learning and conscious processing.

I am a Ph.D. student of Prof. Doina Precup and Prof. Yoshua Bengio, at School of Computer Science of McGill University. In summer 2020, I graduated as a MSc., under Prof. Doina Precup and Prof. Xiao-Wen Chang, with CGPA 4.0/4.0.

I work on aspects of conscious processing in reinforcement learning with Prof. Yoshua Bengio and Prof. Doina Precup.

I graduated as an undergrad from Dalian University of Technology, with major in Computer Science and Technology, and minor in Creative Practices (robotics). I received the award of Distinguished Student Researcher (the only one of class 2018) and graduated with 90+ percentage (CGPA 4.0/4.0).

I had been studying saxophone performance, before switching to science & engineering.
